About

H.A.Y. Travels has been created to empower the Pakistani traveler with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. It aims to deliver value by offering a range of travel products with the highest standard of service along with cutting edge technology. Based in Lahore, Pakistan, H.A.Y. Travels is a one-stop shop for all travel related services. A leading consolidator of travel products, H.A.Y. Travels provides reservation facility for domestic cities. With dedicated customer support operating 24/7 and offices in 3 cities across Pakistan, H.A.Y. Travels is there for you, whenever and wherever.

Find bus timetable and buy your bus ticket online, long-distance and international buses, travelling with bus is easy, cheap, fast and comfortable. Our determination to succeed and our dedication to serve again proved to be our asset and with ever increasing customer base we our serving to seven different destinations and plan to extend to more in recent future.
